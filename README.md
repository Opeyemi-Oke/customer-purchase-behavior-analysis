Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis (Bike Sales Dataset)

Project Overview
This project analyzes customer demographic and socioeconomic data to identity patterns influencing bike purchase decisions. The goal is to uncover insights that can help businesses target the right customers and improve marketing strategies

Dataset Description
The dataset includes:

- Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Marital Status)
- Income level
- ID
- Children
- Education
- Occupation
- Cars
- Commute distance
- Region
- Homeowner
- Purchase decision (Bike Purchased: Yes/No)

Objectives
- Understand key factors influencing bike purchases  
- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Analyze demographic impact on buying decisions

Tools Used
- Excel (Data cleaning & analysis)  
- Pivot Tables
- Slicers
- Charts & dashboards  

Key Insights
- Higher income customers are more likely to purchase bikes  
- Customers with shorter commute distances strongly influences buying of bikes
- Young to middle age adults show higher buying tendency  
- Customer characteristics such as region and education, marital status influences buying patterns  

Business Value
- Helps businesses target the right customer segments  
- Improves marketing strategy and conversion rates  
- Supports data-driven decision-making
